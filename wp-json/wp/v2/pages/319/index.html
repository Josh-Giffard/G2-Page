This privacy policy outlines how we collect, store and use data and information in connection with your use of our website, g2accounting.com.au. It does not apply to any other interactions you may have with us, including for example sending us an email, or our interactions with you as a client.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

Information we collect<\/h2>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

At G2 Accounting, we do not use our website to collect any personally identifiable information about you other than if you choose to submit your details when making an online booking. Any details collected as part of that process are collected and stored through Timely, which you can find out more about from gettimely.com.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

Our website does use Google Analytics to collect information that is not personally identifiable information. Google Analytics uses \u2018cookies\u2019 and a JavaScript code that is contained within the pages on our website, which is designed to collect data and information about your visit and send that information to Google\u2019s servers.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

The information collected by Google is not personal user information, but is instead used to compile statistical reports on our website information. These statistical reports help us understand the browsing behaviour and the general aggregated profile of our visitors, so that we can better tailor our website to give you and future users a better experience when visiting g2accounting.com.au.<\/p>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

Understanding more about Google Analytics<\/h2>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n

To understand more about what data is collected by Google Analytics, how it is stored and how it is used, please visit\u00a0www.google.com\/analytics\/learn\/privacy.html<\/a>.
